Joonkyu Min

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am currently a research intern at Cognitive Learning for Vision and Robotics (CLVR) Lab at KAIST, where I work on robotics under supervision of Joseph J. Lim.

Previously, I worked as a research intern at SNU VGI Lab under professor Jaesik Park, where I worked on 3D computer vision, especially 3D gaussian splatting.

Research Goal

My research goal is to build safe and reliable physical intelligence.

I am particularly interested in robot safety, safe exploration, and uncertainty estimation for reliable real-world robot training and deployment.

One of my long-term goals is to automate science laboratories. By enabling robots to assist with repetitive and labor-intensive lab tasks, I wish it would accelerate scientific discovery across fields such as biotechnology and the life sciences.
